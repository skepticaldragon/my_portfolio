MY 3D GAME: MORBUS

Steam banner created in Photoshop

MORBUS

'Morbus' is the second project I have done during my first year at NCN. The objective for the project was to create a 3D game that included spatial storytelling: a way of puting a story across to the player without directly telling the story. There is no aim to the game apart from trying to piece together all the information that is given to figure out what had happened.
